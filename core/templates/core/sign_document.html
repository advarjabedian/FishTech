This Subscription Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into between FishTech ("Provider") and {{ tenant.name }} ("Customer") as of the date of electronic signature below.

1. Services

Provider agrees to provide Customer access to the FishTech cloud-based platform ("Platform"), which includes compliance management tools, HACCP documentation, daily inspection tracking, order management, and related operational features for seafood processing facilities.

2. Subscription Term

This Agreement is on a month-to-month basis. The subscription automatically renews at the beginning of each billing period unless canceled by either party with at least 30 days written notice before the next billing date.

3. Fees and Payment

  • Customer agrees to pay the monthly subscription fee as quoted at the time of enrollment.
  • Payment is due at the beginning of each monthly billing period via the designated payment method (credit card / ACH).
  • Provider reserves the right to adjust pricing with 30 days advance written notice.
  • If payment is not received within 15 days of the due date, Provider may suspend access to the Platform until payment is received.

4. Customer Responsibilities

  • Customer is solely responsible for the accuracy and completeness of all data entered into the Platform, including but not limited to HACCP plans, inspection records, and compliance documentation.
  • Customer shall maintain the confidentiality of account credentials and is responsible for all activity under their account.
  • Customer shall comply with all applicable local, state, and federal regulations regarding food safety and processing.

5. Data Ownership

All data entered by Customer into the Platform remains the property of Customer. Provider acts as a data processor and will not sell, share, or disclose Customer data to third parties except as required by law or as necessary to provide the services.

6. Limitation of Liability

  • The Platform is a tool to assist with compliance management. Provider does not guarantee regulatory compliance and is not liable for any fines, penalties, or enforcement actions resulting from Customer's operations.
  • Provider's total liability under this Agreement shall not exceed the fees paid by Customer in the preceding 3 months.
  • Provider is not liable for indirect, incidental, or consequential damages.

7. Service Availability

Provider will use commercially reasonable efforts to maintain Platform availability. Provider is not liable for downtime caused by scheduled maintenance (with advance notice), force majeure events, or third-party service interruptions.

8. Termination

  • Either party may terminate this Agreement with 30 days written notice.
  • Upon termination, Customer may request an export of their data within 30 days. After 30 days, Provider may delete Customer data.
  • Provider may terminate immediately if Customer breaches this Agreement or fails to make payment for 30+ days.

9. Governing Law

This Agreement shall be governed by and construed in accordance with the laws of the State of California. Any disputes shall be resolved in the courts of Los Angeles County, California.

This Service Level Agreement ("SLA") is between FishTech ("Provider") and {{ tenant.name }} ("Customer") and supplements the Subscription Agreement.

1. Platform Availability

  • Provider targets 99.5% monthly uptime for the Platform, measured as total minutes in a calendar month minus downtime, divided by total minutes.
  • Scheduled maintenance windows (typically performed during off-peak hours, 10 PM - 4 AM PST) are excluded from uptime calculations.

2. Support

  • Email Support: Available Monday-Friday, 8 AM - 5 PM PST. Response within 24 business hours.
  • Critical Issues (Platform completely unavailable): Response within 4 hours during business hours.
  • Non-Critical Issues (feature not working as expected): Response within 1 business day.
  • Feature Requests: Acknowledged within 3 business days; prioritized based on customer impact.

3. Data Backup

  • Provider performs daily automated backups of all customer data.
  • Backups are retained for a minimum of 30 days.
  • In the event of data loss, Provider will restore from the most recent backup at no additional cost.

4. Incident Communication

In the event of unplanned downtime or a security incident, Provider will:

  • Notify Customer via email within 2 hours of becoming aware of the issue.
  • Provide status updates every 4 hours until resolution.
  • Provide a post-incident summary within 5 business days.

5. Remedies

If monthly uptime falls below 99.5%, Customer may request a service credit equal to 10% of that month's subscription fee for each additional 1% of downtime, up to a maximum credit of one month's fee.

6. Exclusions

This SLA does not apply to downtime caused by:

  • Customer's internet connectivity or hardware issues.
  • Force majeure events (natural disasters, pandemics, etc.).
  • Third-party service outages beyond Provider's control.
  • Actions or inactions of Customer or their authorized users.
