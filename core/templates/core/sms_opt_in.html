How It Works
  1. Text your order to your supplier's dedicated phone number
  2. Your order is received and processed by the team
  3. You may receive confirmation or follow-up texts

SMS Consent & Terms

By texting our order line, you agree to the following:

  • Message Frequency: You may receive order confirmations and occasional updates. Message frequency varies.
  • Message & Data Rates: Standard message and data rates may apply.
  • Opt-Out: Reply STOP at any time to stop receiving messages.
  • Help: Reply HELP for assistance or contact your sales representative.
  • Privacy: Your phone number will only be used for order-related communications. See our Privacy Policy.
