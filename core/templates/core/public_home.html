What We Do

Everything a seafood facility needs

From the receiving dock to regulatory audits, FishTeck covers the full operational lifecycle of a modern seafood processing facility.

HACCP Documentation

Full Hazard Analysis and Critical Control Point documentation. Manage hazard analyses, CCPs, flow charts, and certificates — organized by facility and product type.

Daily Inspections

Pre-Op, Mid-Day, and Post-Op shift inspections with digital signatures, deviation tracking, and corrective action documentation. Replace paper logs entirely.

Purchase Orders

Receive and verify incoming product with temperature logging, lot tracking, and condition inspection. Attach vendor documents and invoices directly to each PO.

Sales Orders

Manage customer orders with full line-item detail. Attach proof of delivery documents and share files with customers via email directly from the platform.

Order Communications

Receive customer orders via email or SMS. Assign order requests to team members, track status, and respond — all from a centralized communication hub.

Reports & Audit Trails

Generate operational and deviation reports on demand. Every action is logged with timestamps and user signatures — ready for any regulatory audit.

Regulatory Compliance

Built around FDA food safety requirements

FishTeck was built from the ground up to align with 21 CFR Part 123 — the FDA's seafood HACCP regulation.

  • Hazard Analysis & CCPs Document every hazard, critical control point, critical limit, monitoring procedure, and corrective action for each product type.
  • Signed Verification Records Every inspection and HACCP document is signed with a digital signature, timestamped, and stored with full audit trail.
  • Deviation & Corrective Action Log All deviations are captured with mandatory corrective action documentation — exactly what auditors look for.
  • HACCP Certificates & Letters of Guarantee Generate and store HACCP certificates and letters of guarantee for customers on demand.
