Digital Ordering

Receive orders by text, email, and web

Your customers order however they want — FishTeck captures it all in one place. No more lost voicemails, missed texts, or buried emails.

SMS / Text Orders

Customers text their orders to your dedicated number. FishTeck automatically transcribes and creates order requests — ready for your team to review and confirm.

Email Orders

Incoming email orders are automatically pulled into FishTeck. Attachments, line items, and customer details are parsed and organized — no manual data entry.

Website Orders

Give customers a clean online ordering portal. They browse your product catalog, build their order, and submit — it flows straight into your order queue.

Digital Compliance

HACCP plans and SOPs, always audit-ready

Replace binders and spreadsheets with a compliance platform built around FDA seafood HACCP requirements and your facility's standard operating procedures.

HACCP Documentation

Full Hazard Analysis and Critical Control Point documentation. Manage hazard analyses, CCPs, flow charts, and certificates — organized by facility and product type.

SOP Inspections

Pre-Op, Mid-Day, and Post-Op shift inspections with digital signatures, deviation tracking, and corrective action documentation. Replace paper logs entirely.

Reports & Audit Trails

Generate deviation and corrective action reports on demand. Every action is logged with timestamps and user signatures — ready for any regulatory audit.

Digital Filing

Invoices, licenses, and documents — organized

Stop digging through file cabinets. Every document your business needs is stored, searchable, and shareable from one central location.

Invoices & Purchase Orders

Attach invoices directly to purchase orders and sales orders. Track vendor documents, proof of delivery, and billing records — all linked to the right transaction.

Licenses & Permits

Store business licenses, health permits, wholesale permits, and certifications. Get notified before they expire so you never operate with a lapsed license.

Customer & Vendor Files

Organize documents by customer or vendor. Share files via email directly from the platform — certificates, spec sheets, or any document they need.
