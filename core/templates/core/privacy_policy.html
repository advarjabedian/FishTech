Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2025

Information We Collect

When you use our SMS ordering service, we collect:

  • Your phone number
  • The content of your text messages (orders)
  • Date and time of messages

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Process and fulfill your orders
  • Send order confirmations and updates
  • Respond to your inquiries
  • Improve our services

Information Sharing

We do not sell or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes. We may share information with:

  • Service providers who help us operate our business (e.g., Twilio for SMS)
  • Legal authorities when required by law

Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure.

Your Rights

You can:

  • Opt out of SMS messages by replying STOP
  • Request access to your data
  • Request deletion of your data
